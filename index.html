Our Inspection Services

Comprehensive inspection solutions tailored to meet the diverse needs of construction projects, from residential to commercial developments.

UCE Program

Preparation of UCE Program according to P130-2025 and CR0, adapted to the type of construction, frequencies, responsibilities and work forms.

Current tracking

Periodic visits, observation sheets and images, completion of the Event Diary. Annual report and prioritization of interventions.

Special tracking

Activated after significant degradations or events. Findings sheet + immediate recommendations and UCE program update.

Due diligence & reception

Indicative checks for transactions or pre-reception: visible non-conformities, recommendations and budgets.

Integration with expertise & design

For advanced degradations: technical expertise, measures project, special follow-up. Multidisciplinary coordination.
